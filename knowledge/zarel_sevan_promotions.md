## Frequently Asked Questions — Promotions

**Is there a discount for buying more than one?**
Yes. Buy 2 bottles for 10% off, or buy 3 bottles for 15% off. Any scent combination counts.

**I want to buy more than 3 bottles — how do I get the best deal?**
We recommend buying in batches of 3. Place one order of 3 bottles to enjoy 15% off, then place a separate new order for the next 3 bottles (4th, 5th, 6th) to enjoy 15% off again. This way you save the most on every purchase.

**What is the maximum I should add to one order?**
3 bottles per order is the sweet spot — you get 15% off and the best savings. Adding more than 3 to a single order will not increase the discount, so split into separate orders of 3 to keep saving.

**I want to buy 6 bottles — how should I order?**
Place two separate orders of 3 bottles each. Both orders qualify for 15% off, so you save on all 6 bottles.

**Is the bundle discount applied automatically?**
Yes. The discount is applied automatically at checkout on TikTok Shop or zarelsevan.my when you have the qualifying quantity in your cart. No code needed.

**Can I mix scents in one bundle order?**
Yes. Any combination of Emerald Apple, Citrus Zeste, and Velvety Peach counts toward the bundle. Mix freely.

**Do you have any promo codes?**

**Can I combine a promo code with a bundle deal?**
Yes. Bundle deals are a permanent promotion and can be combined with active promo codes. If there is a running promotion, you can apply the promo code on top of your bundle discount in the same order.
