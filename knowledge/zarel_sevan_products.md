## How to Use

For best results, soak the rattan reed sticks in the perfume oil for 15 minutes before first use. This allows the sticks to fully absorb the oil and open up the reeds, ensuring a stronger and more consistent scent diffusion from the start. After soaking, flip the sticks so the dry end faces up — this refreshes the fragrance and extends the life of the diffuser.

## Longevity

Each 50ml bottle lasts approximately **60 days** under normal conditions (indoor use, moderate air circulation).
